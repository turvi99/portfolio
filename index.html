ABOUT ME

AI/ML Researcher · Published Author · Developer

I'm Turvi Devineni, a B.Tech student in Computer Science (AI & ML Specialization) at SRM University AP, Amaravati. My research focuses on building lightweight, efficient deep learning frameworks for real-world applications — particularly in medical imaging and cross-domain adaptation.

My published work, ClinFuse-CXR, demonstrates how a 12M-parameter multimodal framework can rival 200M+ parameter models like BioViL and MedCLIP. I'm passionate about making AI accessible, efficient, and deployable in resource-constrained environments.

📄 COMSYS 2026 — Published

CLINFUSE-CXR

Susmitha et al., Turvi Devineni, Abhigna T., Akbar B.S., Ajaykumar S., Raushan K.S.

A lightweight multimodal framework for chest X-ray diagnosis fusing EfficientNet-B0 + Bio_ClinicalBERT via cross-modal attention. Only 16M trainable parameters — rivals models 10× its size. Demonstrated zero-shot cross-dataset generalization from MIMIC-CXR to CheXpert.
